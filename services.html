Our Services

Max Rehab Services

Our aim at Max Rehab is to treat your injury with expert care. Our team offer a range of services in order to achieve your goals. Our chartered physiotherapists will work with you to to diagnose the cause of your pain and design a unique treatment plan.

Services we offer

Your Visit

Visiting Max Rehab

Initial Consultation

Arrive early on your first visit as you will need to fill out an initial consultation form. Your therapist will bring you into a treatment room to assess you. This assessment involves explaining your issue as well as a physical assessment performed by your therapist. After the assessment, your phyiotherapist will develop a treatment plan for you, which will be started in the first session.

Planning Your Treatment

After your assessment, your personalised treatment will begin. Treatment usually involves one or more of the following:

  • hands-on manipulation/manual therapy
  • massage therapy
  • electrotherapy
  • dry needling
  • advice and education

Depending on your injury, your therapist may perscribe an exercise plan for you to work on at home. These exercises will be explained to you during your session and you will be emailed a description of the plan.

Follow-up Appointments

Depending on the extend of your injury and how your body re-acts to treatment, you may be advised by your therapist to book a follow-up appointment. The length of time between treatments is completely case depended - put your trust in the advice of our physiotherapists!

If you do require a follow-up appointment, you will be quickly re-assessed at the beginning any subsequent visits. The physio will check your progress since the previous visit and adjust your treatment plan accordingly. Rest assured, you will always be in the best hands at Max Rehab.
