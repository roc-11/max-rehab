How can we help you?

Online Physio

Max Rehab offers clients online consultations. One of our Chartered Physiotherapists will provide diagnosis, advice and a recovery programme from the comfort of your home.

Post-op Rehab

We provide expert treatment and recovery plans for post-operative procedures. Book one of our physiotherapists to help you regain strength and movement following your procedure.

Physiotherapy

Meet with one of our physiotherapists on site in Cork. Our aim is to build a personalised programme to suit your body's individual needs and help you reach your goals.

Pain Management

Living with a long term injury? Max Rehab can assess your injury and create a bespoke plan to reduce pain and inflammation.
