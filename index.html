Max Rehab Physiotherapy

How can we help you?

Online Physio


Max Rehab offers clients online consultations. Meet with one of our Chartered Physiotherapists from the comfort of your own home. Our physiotherapists will provide diagnosis, advice and a recovery programme with this service.

Post-op Rehab


We provide expert treatment and recovery plans for post-operative procedures. Book one of our physiotherapists to help you regain strength and movement following your procedure.

Physiotherapy


Meet with one of our physiotherapists on site in Cork. Our aim is to build a personalised programme to suit your body's individual needs. Whether you need to recover from an injury, manage pain or rehab after surgery, our experienced staff can help you reach your goals.

Pain Management


Living with a long term injury? Max Rehab can assess your injury and create a bespoke plan to reduce pain and inflammation.
